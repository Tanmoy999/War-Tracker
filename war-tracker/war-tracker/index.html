ℹ️ About Conflict Tracker
🎯

Our Mission

Conflict Tracker is an independent, open-source data portal providing real-time statistics, interactive visualizations, and a comprehensive timeline of the 2025-2026 Iran-Israel-US conflict. We make conflict data accessible, transparent, and understandable.

📊

Methodology

We aggregate data from 20+ verified international sources including Reuters, Al Jazeera, CENTCOM, UN OCHA, WHO, UNHCR, and MSF. All figures are cross-referenced before publication and updated every 60 seconds.

⚠️

Disclaimer

This is an informational portal only. We are not affiliated with any government or military. Casualty figures are contested by all sides and change rapidly during active conflict. Always verify from official sources.
